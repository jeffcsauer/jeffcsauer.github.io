Welcome to the new and improved (i.e. streamlined) site! After many hours of chasing what I thought thought was a good website, I realized that simplicity and scalability was key. This site will act as a one-stop-shop for a brief biosketch, blog posts, class materials, a regularly updated CV, and noteworthy news.

In pursuit of efficiency, I switched from the Hugo Academic theme to the Hugo Xmin theme written by the prodigious Yihui Xie. At its core, Yihui states that ‘This XMin theme contains about 130 lines of code in total, including the code in HTML templates and CSS (also counting empty lines).’

I am actively looking for collaborations! I currently live in the DC area but regularly travel to NYC.
